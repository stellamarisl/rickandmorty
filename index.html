Rick and Morty is the Emmy award-winning half-hour animated hit comedy series on Adult Swim that follows a sociopathic genius scientist.
Rick is a drunken, brilliant scientist who kidnaps his cantankerous teenage grandson Morty for adventures in other worlds and alternate dimensions.
Created By: Dan Harmon, Justin Roiland

Made with patience and enthusiasm by Stella Maris Loreto and Claudia Rocha
